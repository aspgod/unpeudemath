In my last game theory class I gave students the choice between starting to rigorously look at extensive form games or to look at the last play of this years superbowl.
No one objected to looking at the superbowl play (which has been called 'the worst play in superbowl history') as an opportunity to go over all the normal form game theory we have seen so far.
